## Notes

- These counts replace the regex-based numbers in `audit/type-safety/README.md`.
- The peer review predicted non-null assertions were ~2.5× under-counted by the regex; `no-non-null-assertion` here is the authoritative count.
- `no-unsafe-*` rules count downstream consequences of `any` (e.g. `row.x` access where `row: any`). Fixing one `any` at a boundary collapses many of these.
- Reduction targets in remediation PRs should track `no-explicit-any`, `no-non-null-assertion`, and `no-unsafe-*` separately — they have different costs and meanings.
